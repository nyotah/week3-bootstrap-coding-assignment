Ready to find the right houseplant for you?

Let's go over some common types of houseplants, each requiring various levels of care.

Monsterra

Monstera plants, known for their striking split leaves, are low-maintenance and perfect for beginners. They thrive in bright, indirect light and only need watering when the top inch of soil feels dry.

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Pothos

Pothos are one of the easiest houseplants to care for, making them ideal for beginners. They tolerate low light and irregular watering, and their trailing vines add a lush, lively touch to any space.
